# Appointment Form

## Identification

- **Title** — appointment name (e.g., *Technical visit — plumbing*, *Contract signing*)
- **Description** — details, access instructions, required materials, etc.

## Date and time

- **Date** — day of the appointment
- **Time** — start time

## Status

| Status | When to use |
|---|---|
| **Scheduled** | Future or open appointment |
| **Completed** | Completed — fill in the completion notes |
| **Cancelled** | Not completed |

## Optional links

All optional — fill in the relevant ones:

- **Property** — property where the appointment will take place
- **Contract/Booking** — related rental contract
- **Guest** — involved tenant
- **Service Provider** — responsible vendor

> Linking the service provider makes it easier to track maintenance visits and service history per vendor.

## Completion notes

Available when marking status as **Completed**. Record what was done, issues found, or next steps — serves as a maintenance history for the property.
