# Guest Form

## Identification

- **Full name** — civil name; appears in contracts and reports
- **Email** — primary contact; used as a variable in contract templates
- **Phone** — with area code; free format
- **Nationality** — relevant for contracts with foreign tenants
- **ID / Document** — primary identification document

## Documents

Add multiple documents (ID, CPF, passport, driver's license, etc.), each with type and number.

## Address

Full address for contractual and billing purposes.

## Notes

Internal notes about the guest — do not appear in documents.

## Guarantors (Sponsors)

Add people who guarantee payment in case of default. Each guarantor has their own name, email, phone, and documents.

> Guarantors appear automatically in contract templates that use the corresponding variable.

## Dependents

Add family members or companions who live with the tenant. Useful for occupant control and documentation.

## CSV import

To register multiple guests at once, use **Import CSV** in the Guests screen. Available fields: name, email, phone, address, nationality, marital status, profession, date of birth, document type and number.
