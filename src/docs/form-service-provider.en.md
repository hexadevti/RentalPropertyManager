# Service Provider Form

## Identification

- **Name** — full name (freelancer) or company name
- **Service type** — main specialty (e.g., *Electrical*, *Plumbing*, *Cleaning*, *Painting*, *General*)

> Use standardized service names to make searching easier when scheduling maintenance.

## Contact

- **Phone** — with area code; primary contact number
- **Email** — for sending requests and quotes

## Fiscal documentation

- **Tax ID** — required for invoice and service contract issuance

## Address

Provider or company address — for correspondence and formal contracts.

## Notes

Use this field for:
- Service rating after each job
- Average prices charged
- Access time restrictions
- Contact for a specific person

## CSV import

To register multiple service providers at once, use **Import CSV** in the Service Providers screen.
