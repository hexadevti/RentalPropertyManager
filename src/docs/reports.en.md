# Reports

## What is this screen?

Consolidates data from all modules into analytical charts and tables to support portfolio decisions. Reports are generated dynamically based on the selected period.

## Period selection

- **Quick presets** — Last 3, 6, or 12 months
- **Custom period** — manually set start and end dates

## Available panels

### Financial evolution
Bar chart with monthly income and expenses. Visualize trends and seasonality.

### Expense distribution
Pie chart with expenses grouped by category. Identify where money is being spent.

### Revenue by property
Horizontal bar chart with revenue generated by each property. Identify the most profitable properties.

### Occupancy rate
Percentage of days with an active contract relative to total days in the analyzed period.

### Expiring contracts/bookings
List of contracts ending within the next 30 days — requires attention for renewal or closure.

### Upcoming appointments
Schedule of upcoming appointments registered in the **Appointments** module.

### Task completion trend
Chart showing the evolution of tasks created and completed in the period.

### Top service providers
Ranking of providers by number of services and total cost in the period.

### Most active guests
List of guests with the most contracts and financial transactions in the period.

## Tips

> Reports only reflect data with dates within the selected period.

> For more detailed natural language analysis, use the **AI Assistant** — it accesses the same data and can calculate balances, list contracts, and compare periods.

> Occupancy rate is calculated per property based on linked active contracts.
