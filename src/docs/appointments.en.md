# Appointments

## What is this screen?

Records and tracks appointments and visits related to the portfolio — scheduled inspections, maintenance visits, tenant meetings, service provider appointments, etc.

## Form fields

- **Title** — appointment name
- **Description** — details and notes
- **Date** — day of the appointment
- **Time** — start time
- **Status** — Scheduled, Completed, or Cancelled
- **Property** *(optional)* — related property
- **Contract/Booking** *(optional)* — associated contract
- **Guest** *(optional)* — involved tenant
- **Service Provider** *(optional)* — responsible vendor

## Appointment status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Scheduled** | Future or open appointment |
| **Completed** | Successfully completed |
| **Cancelled** | Not completed |

## Completion with notes

When marking an appointment as **Completed**, a **Completion notes** field is shown to record what was done, observations found, or next steps.

## Available filters

- **Search** — by title or description
- **Status** — filter by situation
- **Period** — upcoming or by date range

## Calendar integration

All appointments appear in the **Calendar** on the corresponding day, providing an integrated view with contracts and tasks.

## Tips

> Link the service provider to the appointment to facilitate maintenance visit tracking.

> Use **Completion notes** to record what was done — it serves as a maintenance history for the property.

> Upcoming appointments appear in the **Reports** panel in the schedule section.
