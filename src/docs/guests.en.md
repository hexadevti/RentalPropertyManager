# Guests

## What is this screen?

Centralizes the registration of guests and tenants — the people who sign rental contracts/bookings. Includes personal data, documentation, contact info, and family links (guarantors and dependents).

## Main fields

### Identification
- **Full name** — civil name of the guest
- **Email** — used for communication and as a contract identifier
- **Phone** — mobile or landline with area code
- **Nationality** — important for contracts with foreign tenants
- **ID / Document** — primary identification document

### Address
Full address for contractual and billing purposes.

### Notes
Free text for internal notes about the guest.

## Guarantors and dependents

| Link | Description |
|---|---|
| **Guarantor** | Person who guarantees payment in case of default |
| **Dependent** | Family member or companion who lives with the tenant |

> Guarantors appear automatically in contract templates that use the corresponding variable.

## CSV import

Use the **Import CSV** button to import multiple guests at once. Download the template, fill it in, and import. Available fields: name, email, phone, address, nationality, marital status, profession, date of birth, document type and number.

## Search and filter

Use the search bar to find guests by name, email, or phone.

## Tips

> Keep the email updated — it is used as an identifier in contract/booking templates.

> Register guarantors before creating a contract if the template uses a guarantors variable.

> Guest contract/booking history can be checked by filtering contracts by guest in the **Contracts/Bookings** screen.
